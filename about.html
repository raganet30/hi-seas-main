Est. 2019

Our Company

Hi-Seas Philippines began its operations in June 2019 with market research, followed by the establishment of its first business unit in July 2019 (Non-Trenching). The company was officially registered as HI-SEAS CO. LTD. INC. in September 2019. Since then, it has earned major accreditations such as the PCAB license (March 2020) and the VAS Certificate (October 2022).
